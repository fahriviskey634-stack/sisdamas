# Domain Knowledge
## Core Business Domain Details for Desa Sukahaji

This file records the specific domain requirements for the Desa Sukahaji deployment.

### 1. Geographic Boundaries
Desa Sukahaji, Kecamatan Cipendeuy, Kabupaten Bandung Barat.
*   **Target Scope:** Only Dusun 2 boundary.
*   **Dusun 2 Divisions:** Divided into 3 RWs (RW 01, RW 02, RW 03).
*   **RT Divisions:** Divided into 9 RTs.
*   **Estimated Households:** ~120 households to be surveyed.

### 2. Survey Taxonomy (Starter Taxonomy)
The problem and potential categorizations must fall under one of these 7 standard categories to ensure consistency during skoring:
1.  **Infrastruktur:** Roads, drainage, clean water supply, public halls, waste management.
2.  **Kesehatan:** Posyandu frequency, stunting cases, public health clinics access.
3.  **Pendidikan:** School access, literacy, community learning centers.
4.  **Ekonomi:** Household income, local UMKM, farming potentials.
5.  **Lingkungan:** Landslides risk, sanitation, environmental cleanliness.
6.  **Sosial-Budaya:** Youth activities, religious gatherings, cultural events.
7.  **Keamanan:** Neighborhood security posts, night watch organization.

### 3. Skoring USG (Prioritisation)
*   **U (Urgency):** How urgent is it to resolve the issue?
*   **S (Seriousness):** What is the impact if the issue is left alone?
*   **G (Growth):** How fast will it worsen if not addressed now?
*   *Scale:* 1 (lowest) to 5 (highest). Total score is `U + S + G` (range 3 to 15).
*   High USG scores trigger Program plans in Cycle 4.
