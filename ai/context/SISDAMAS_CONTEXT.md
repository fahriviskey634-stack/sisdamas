# SISDAMAS Context
## Sistem Pemberdayaan Masyarakat (SISDAMAS) Methodology

This file documents the methodology details for the system.

### 1. The Four Cycles of SISDAMAS
1.  **Siklus 1 (Sosialisasi & Rembug Warga):** Community socialization and gathering sessions. Physical sticky notes (yellow for aspirations, red for problems, green for potentials) are digitised onto the Digital Sticky Notes Board on Day 2 of KKN.
2.  **Siklus 2 (Refleksi Sosial & Pemetaan):** Door-to-door household surveys to map housing conditions, collect precise GPS locations, verify problems/potentials, and capture photos. Survey sprint runs on Day 4–8.
3.  **Siklus 3 (Perencanaan Partisipatif):** Classification and skoring of problems using the Urgency, Seriousness, and Growth (USG) method in the Priority Matrix.
4.  **Siklus 4 (Pelaksanaan Program & Dokumentasi):** Implementation of community development programs addressing ranked problems. Progress tracked via program tasks, milestone dates, and photo uploads, which are synced to Google Drive/Calendar.

### 2. Data Philosophy
*   Every survey is associated with a household unit. 
*   A household unit represents a unique location containing a family.
*   Data collected serves to generate reports for UIN SGD campus (Laporan Penanggung Jawaban / LPJ).
