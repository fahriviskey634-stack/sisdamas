# User Personas
## Deployed Roles on the SISDAMAS Digital Platform

### 1. Super Administrator (1 account)
*   **Who:** The solo developer/student builder.
*   **Access:** Full control. Manages master data (Dusun, RW, RT), batch user creation, Google service accounts configurations, audit logs review, and database soft-delete recovery.

### 2. KKN Team Member (15 accounts)
*   **Who:** The KKN student surveyors.
*   **Access:** Authenticated member role. Accesses dashboard, logs sticky notes during Cycle 1, conducts surveys, captures coordinates, saves offline drafts, and edits their own survey entries.

### 3. Public Visitor (Unauthenticated)
*   **Who:** Village officials (RT/RW chiefs, Kadus, Village Head), village residents, supervising lecturer (DPL), and the public.
*   **Access:** No login. Accesses public landing page, news, gallery, anonymized map (shifted coordinates, PII removed), and aggregated charts dashboard.
