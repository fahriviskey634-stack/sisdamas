# FIELD USABILITY REVIEW

Evaluate the UX specifically for field conditions.

Assume users are:

- Standing outdoors.
- Walking between houses.
- Under direct sunlight.
- Wearing masks or hats.
- Holding documents in one hand.
- Using Android phones with one hand.
- Experiencing unstable internet.
- Low battery conditions.
- Occasionally losing GPS signal.

Review every workflow.

Suggest UX improvements specifically for field surveys.

Prioritize speed, simplicity, and reliability over visual complexity.
